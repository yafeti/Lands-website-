5 Foods Rich in Iron

Boost Your Energy and Prevent Anemia

Iron is essential for producing hemoglobin, which carries oxygen in your blood. Many people suffer from iron deficiency without knowing it. Here are 5 foods that are excellent sources of iron:

  1. Spinach: Packed with non-heme iron, great in salads, smoothies, or cooked dishes.
  2. Beans: Kidney beans, lentils, and chickpeas provide a healthy iron boost.
  3. Red Meat: Beef and liver are rich in heme iron, which is absorbed efficiently by the body.
  4. Fortified Cereals: Many breakfast cereals are enriched with iron, perfect for a morning start.
  5. Tofu: A plant-based source of iron for vegetarians and vegans.

Tip: Pair iron-rich foods with vitamin C sources (like oranges or tomatoes) to enhance absorption.
