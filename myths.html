Nutrition Myth Buster

Welcome to the Nutrition Myth Buster! Here we separate facts from fiction and help you make informed choices for a healthier life.

Myth 1: Carbs Make You Fat

Fact: Carbohydrates are a primary energy source for your body. Overeating any macronutrient can lead to weight gain, not carbs alone. Focus on quality sources like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.

Myth 2: Eating Fat is Bad for You

Fact: Healthy fats are essential for your body, including brain function and hormone production. Choose unsaturated fats from sources like avocado, nuts, seeds, and fish.

Myth 3: Skipping Meals Helps You Lose Weight

Fact: Skipping meals can slow metabolism and lead to overeating later. Eating balanced meals regularly supports healthy weight management.
