Hydration Tips for a Healthy Life

Stay Energized and Healthy Every Day

Water is essential for digestion, metabolism, and maintaining body temperature. Here are practical ways to stay hydrated:

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily; carry a reusable bottle.
  • Eat water-rich fruits and vegetables like watermelon, cucumber, and oranges.
  • Limit sugary drinks and caffeine, which can dehydrate your body.
  • Set reminders on your phone if you often forget to drink water.

Staying hydrated improves concentration, digestion, and overall well-being. Make it a daily habit!
