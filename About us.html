About Us

LUANAR Association of Nutrition and Dietetics Students (LANDS)

The LUANAR Association of Nutrition and Dietetics Students (LANDS) is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting nutrition education, research, and community engagement.

Our members are passionate about improving health and wellness through balanced diets, evidence-based nutrition, and public awareness.

Our Mission

To empower nutrition and dietetics students with practical knowledge, leadership skills, and a strong professional network to promote good nutrition and health in Malawi and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a leading student association that inspires and prepares future nutrition professionals to create a healthier nation.
